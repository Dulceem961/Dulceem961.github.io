Travel to Japan with me

Japan is one of the richest countries in tradition on the Asian continent. Temples, Zen gardens lost between mountains and Shinto shrines show the spirituality that has always characterized the country.

The best cities to visit

These are the top 3 cities you must visit in your travel to Japan:

  • Tokyo
  • Kyoto
  • Osaka

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the metropolitan area with the most population in the world.

Kyoto

Kyoto is considered by many to be the most beautiful city in Japan. It is home to some of the most emblematic monuments, with some of the most beautiful gardens.

Osaka

Osaka is Japan's third largest city and was the country's first capital. It is considered Japan's booming economic center.
